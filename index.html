Welcome

Toronto Church on the Rock Ministries (TCR) is a multicultural, non-denominational ministry, spreading the fire of God's love, presence and healing in the local church, the community and abroad. We have the mandate to preach, teach, train and raise up leaders, equip the Body of Christ and establish the Kingdom of God wherever God sends us.

At TCR, we believe that Jesus is the answer to all of life's challenges and problems and that God's Word is alive and active and has the power and authority to change and transform lives.

Always remember that Jesus loves you very much and has a wonderful plan for your life. It is his desire to give you hope and a fulfilled future.
